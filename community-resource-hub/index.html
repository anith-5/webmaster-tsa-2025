About

The Alpharetta Resource Hub was created to make it easier for residents to find the community support they need, all in one convenient place. Whether someone is looking for housing assistance, food programs, job training, youth services, or mental health resources, our website brings together verified, local organizations that can offer real help. By organizing these services into clear categories, providing accurate contact information, and offering tools like a resource map and submission form, the Hub allows users to quickly navigate available support and connect with the right programs. Our goal is to simplify the search for assistance and strengthen our community by ensuring that everyone in Alpharetta has access to essential resources when they need them most.
