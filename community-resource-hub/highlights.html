Food-A-Thon 2025

Atlanta Apartment Association

Fighting Food Insecurity

The 38th annual Food-A-Thon represents Metro Atlanta's largest food drive, uniting apartment communities to combat hunger. Through partnerships with Atlanta Community Food Bank, this initiative has delivered over 145 million meals across three decades. Community members collected donations and volunteered to support families facing food insecurity throughout Metro Atlanta and North Georgia. Every contribution helps provide nutritious meals to vulnerable neighbors.

Meal Delivery to Seniors

Open Hand Atlanta

Metro Atlanta Communities

Volunteers delivered medically-tailored, nutritious meals directly to homebound seniors across Metro Atlanta. Open Hand Atlanta prepares over 6,000 meals daily for elderly individuals unable to shop or cook independently. Each delivery brings not only sustenance but also vital human connection to isolated seniors. Volunteer drivers spent quality time ensuring recipients received proper nutrition and compassionate care, making meaningful impacts on vulnerable neighbors' health and wellbeing.

The pantry served dozens of households, offering nutritious options to support family wellbeing. This ongoing initiative strengthens community bonds while addressing immediate hunger needs with compassion and respect for each individual served.
